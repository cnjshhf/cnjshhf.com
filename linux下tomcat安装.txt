1、下载安装文件：Download apache-tomcat-7.0.14.tar.gz   下载

2、输入命令解压文件：tar - zxvf apache-tomcat-7.0.14.tar.gz

3、将文件放到特定目录：mv apache-tomcat-7.0.14 /usr/local/tomcat7.0

4、配置环境变量vim /root/.bash_profile

                          export CATALINA_HOME=/usr/local/tomcat7.0

        export CATALINA_BASH=/usr/local/tomcat7.0

5、启动：   /usr/local/tomcat7/bin/startup.sh
